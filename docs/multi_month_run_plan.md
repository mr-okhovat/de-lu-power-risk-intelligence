# Multi-month run plan

Current baseline month: June 2024.

Additional months:

- May 2024
- July 2024
- August 2024

Purpose:

Check whether the current risk signal behaves consistently across nearby months before changing thresholds or adding a backtest layer.

What to compare after running:

- high-risk row count
- extreme row count
- mean and median risk score
- reason-code distribution
- top risk hours
- whether the signal is too quiet, too noisy, or directionally stable

Current rule:

Do not tune thresholds until at least four monthly windows have been reviewed.
