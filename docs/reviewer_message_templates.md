## Friendly version

Hi [Name],

I’ve brought my DE-LU power-market analytics project to a first reviewer-ready checkpoint and would really value your technical view.

It is a public-data prototype using SMARD/Bundesnetzagentur data. The current version builds hourly market features, an explainable fundamentals-based risk signal, day-ahead price-event labels, and a cross-month signal-vs-price-event evaluation for May to August 2024.

The main result so far: the signal is conservative and fires rarely, but signal-positive hours show higher ex-post price-event concentration than the monthly base rate in all reviewed months.

I am not claiming price forecasting, trading performance, P&L, or production readiness. I mainly want to know whether the framing makes sense from a power trading analytics perspective, and what you would add next: forecast error, balancing/imbalance, weather, cross-border flows, intraday prices, or lead-time evaluation.
